ABOUT US

What is Project Roshan?

Project Roshan is a social initiative focused on providing solar-powered lighting solutions to rural and underprivileged communities. Our goal is to ensure that every household has access to sustainable and reliable energy, empowering them with better education, safety, and livelihood opportunities.

Our Mission!

  • To eliminate energy poverty in underserved areas.
  • To promote clean and affordable energy solutions.
  • To improve education and productivity through access to light.

Why This Project? – The Need for Change

The Problem We Are Solving

  • Millions of people in India still live without electricity.
  • Students struggle to study after sunset.
  • Families use kerosene lamps, which cause pollution and health hazards.

With Project Roshan, we are changing these numbers!

  • 100% solar-powered lighting solutions
  • Cost-effective & sustainable energy
  • Better education & safety for children
  • Let’s make a difference, together!

TOWARDS RASABEDA – OUR FIRST MILESTONE

WHY RASABEDA?

Rasabeda is a small village where electricity is a luxury, and most families rely on candles and oil lamps. Our team identified this community as the first step towards a brighter future.

OUR PLAN

We are setting up a centralized solar panel system that will provide a reliable, sustainable solar-powered connection to every house in Rasabeda.

OUR AIM

50+ homes powered with solar lighting.
Reduction in hazardous fuel usage.
Self-Sustaining, eco-friendly future.
