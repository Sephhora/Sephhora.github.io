The Problems We Solve

Land Degradation & Biodiversity Loss

SDG Connections

Tackling land degradation through SDG 15, supported by climate action (SDG 13) and sustainable practices (SDG 12).

Carbon Offset

SDG Connections

Directly linked to SDG 13 (Climate Action),Supported by SDG 7 (Clean Energy) and SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption) for sustainable carbon management.

Lack of Transparency in Plantation Claims

SDG Connections

Addressed by SDG 12 (Responsible Production) for ethical sourcing and SDG 16 (Transparency) to ensure accountability in environmental claims.

Fragile Rural Livelihoods

SDG Connections

Core focus on SDG 1 (No Poverty) and SDG 8 (Decent Work), with support from SDG 2 (Zero Hunger) for agricultural stability.

Gaps in Government Scheme Convergence

SDG Connections

Addressed by SDG 16 (Strong Institutions) for policy implementation and SDG 17 (Partnerships) for multi-stakeholder coordination.

Untapped Potential of CSR/Climate Finance

SDG Connections

Leverages SDG 13 (Climate Action) funding and SDG 17 (Partnerships) for private sector engagement in sustainable development.
